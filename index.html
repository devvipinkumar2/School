About Our School

Building brighter futures through inclusive education and compassionate care.

Our Mission

Empowering Through Education

M.A.B Andh Vidhyalaya is dedicated to empowering visually impaired students through quality education, fostering independence, and nurturing their unique talents. Located in the heart of Nideban, Maharashtra, we provide a safe, supportive, and enriching environment where every child can thrive. Our school serves students from Grades 1 through 7, offering a comprehensive curriculum adapted to meet the specific needs of visually impaired learners. With dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and 24-hour residential care, we ensure that every student receives the attention and support they deserve.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and excellence in special education, where every child discovers their potential and builds a confident future.

Our Values

Compassion, excellence, inclusivity, and empowerment guide everything we do. We believe in nurturing both academic and personal growth.

Our Community

Students, dedicated staff, and supportive families come together to create a warm, caring environment where everyone belongs.
